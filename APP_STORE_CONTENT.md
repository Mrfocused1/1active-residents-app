## Description (4000 characters max)

Make your community better with Active Residents – the easiest way to report local issues directly to your council.

**REPORT ISSUES IN SECONDS**
Spotted a pothole? Missed bin collection? Broken street light? Report it in just a few taps. Active Residents makes it simple to document and submit community issues to your local council.

**KEY FEATURES**

• Quick Reporting – Report potholes, fly-tipping, broken lights, and more in under a minute
• Photo Evidence – Attach photos to help your council understand the issue
• Precise Location – Pin-point exact locations on the map for faster resolution
• Track Progress – See all community issues on an interactive map
• Council News – Stay updated with news from your local council
• Impact Dashboard – See how your reports make a difference

**HOW IT WORKS**

1. Select Your Issue – Choose from common categories like Roads, Rubbish, Lighting, or Parks
2. Add Details – Take a photo and describe the problem
3. Confirm Location – We'll automatically detect your location or let you adjust it
4. Submit – Your report goes straight to the right department at your council
5. Track – Monitor the status of your report and see when it's fixed

**WHO IS THIS FOR?**

Active Residents is perfect for anyone who cares about their local community:
• Residents who want to improve their neighbourhood
• Community leaders coordinating local improvements
• Council-conscious citizens who want to make a difference
• Anyone frustrated with finding the right council contact

**YOUR VOICE MATTERS**

Every report helps make your community better. Whether it's a small pavement crack or a major fly-tipping incident, your input helps councils prioritize repairs and keep neighbourhoods safe and clean.

**COVERING ALL OF LONDON**

Active Residents works across all 32 London boroughs, from Westminster to Greenwich, Camden to Croydon. No matter where you live in London, you can report issues to your local council.

**SECURE & PRIVATE**

Your personal information is protected. We only share what's necessary for your council to address the issue. No spam, no unnecessary data collection.

**FREE TO USE**

Active Residents is completely free. No subscriptions, no hidden fees. Just a simple tool to help you make your community better.

Download Active Residents today and start making a difference in your neighbourhood!

## Tips for Approval

1. **Be Honest:** Clearly explain what the app does
2. **Provide Demo Account:** Make it easy for reviewers to test
3. **Respond Quickly:** Monitor App Store Connect for reviewer questions
4. **Test Thoroughly:** Ensure no crashes on the reviewer's device
5. **Follow Guidelines:** Review Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
