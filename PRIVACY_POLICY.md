# Privacy Policy for Active Residents

**Last Updated: December 23, 2025**

## Introduction

Active Residents ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register and use Active Residents, we may collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Home address (for accurate report submission)
- Profile photo (optional)

### Location Information
- GPS coordinates when you report an issue
- Borough/council area you're reporting in
- Precise location of reported issues (to send to councils)

### Content You Create
- Photos of reported issues
- Descriptions of problems
- Report submissions

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Submit reports to your local council
- Show you relevant community issues on the map
- Display council news for your area
- Track the status of your reports
- Improve the app and fix bugs

## Information Sharing

### With Councils
We share report information (including location, photos, description, and your contact details) with the relevant local council to address the issue.

### With Service Providers
We may share information with trusted third-party providers who help us host and maintain the app.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct your data
- Delete your account
- Opt-out of communications
